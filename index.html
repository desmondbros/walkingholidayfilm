The Story

An office worker quits his job to walk the Camino de Santiago in his mother’s memory, but takes a detour when he meets a Galician grandmother on a mission to collect the ashes of her dead son.

About Walking Holiday

Walking Holiday is an Irish feature film, currently going to festivals.

• Running time: 70 minutes

• Genre: Drama, Feel-good Roadtrip

• STARRING Amhlaoibh Mac Suibhne & Elena Seijo

• Directed by Cian Desmond & Jack Desmond

• Produced by Silvia Laguna López

• Shot on location in Galicia (Spain) and Cork (Ireland).
